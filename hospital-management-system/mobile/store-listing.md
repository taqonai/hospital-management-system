# HMS Patient Portal - App Store Listing

## App Name
**HMS Patient Portal**

## Short Description (80 characters max)
Manage your health appointments, records, and prescriptions in one secure app.

## Full Description

HMS Patient Portal puts your healthcare at your fingertips. Access your medical records, book appointments, manage prescriptions, and communicate with your healthcare providers - all from your smartphone.

### Key Features

**Appointment Management**
- Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments with ease
- View upcoming and past appointments
- Add appointments directly to your calendar
- Get directions to your healthcare facility

**Medical Records**
- Access your complete medical history
- View lab results with easy-to-understand explanations
- Track abnormal values with visual indicators
- Download and share reports securely

**Prescriptions**
- View all active and past prescriptions
- Request prescription refills
- Set medication reminders
- Track refills remaining

**Health Insights**
- AI-powered health analysis
- Track vital signs trends
- Personalized health recommendations
- Symptom checker with triage guidance

**Billing & Payments**
- View itemized bills
- Track payment history
- Pay bills securely in-app
- Download invoices

**Security & Privacy**
- HIPAA-compliant data protection
- Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
- Auto-logout for your protection
- Encrypted data storage

### Why Choose HMS Patient Portal?

- **Secure**: Your health data is protected with industry-leading security
- **Convenient**: Everything you need in one app
- **Fast**: Quick access to appointments and records
- **Smart**: AI-powered insights to help you stay healthy
